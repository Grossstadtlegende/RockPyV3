Hysteresis¶

class Structure.measurements.Hysteresis(sample, mtype, mfile, machine, mag_method)[source]¶

A subclass of the measurement class. It devides the raw_data give into an down_field and up_field branch. It will also look or a virgibn branch or an \(M_{si}\) branch.

E_delta_t()[source]¶

Method calculates the \(E^{\Delta}_t\) value for the hysteresis. It uses scipy.integrate.simps for calculation of the area under the down_field branch for positive fields and later subtracts the area under the Msi curve.

calculate_bc(poly=5, check=False)[source]¶

Calulates Bc from hysteresis loop. Calculates seperately for up_field and down_field branch. The data gets fitted by a polynomial of degree poly (default = 5).
